After developing and publishing my first interative digital magazine on the iOS platform in 2013 I realized the time had come for me to pursue my passion full-time. I love web development because it is always changing. I really enjoy learning new things and technology allows me to keep learning.

After refreshing my knowledge of the basics of HTML, which I picked up while in college, I learned and developed sites using Wordpress before venturing into other frameworks and languages, like Bootstrap, JavaScript, Python, and Ruby.
