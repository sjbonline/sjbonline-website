Portfolio description

Clear Cut Legal Service is an Attorney's Personal Service Company. They provide legal document delivery, mobile notary public services, and private investigation assistance in the Greater Atlanta Area. The service needed a simple site to explain their services and allow for customer contact. This site is a single page site with a parallax effect created using Bootstrap3, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a bit of jQuery. It also incorporates an API the contact form.
